## Quick Summary

- **Data**: Reference only — does not substitute official sources from Petrolimex, PVOil, or the Ministry of Industry and Trade (MOIT).
- **Source License**: MIT — free to use, copy, modify, and redistribute for any purpose.
- **Scraping**: `VietFuelBot/1.0` operates at a reasonable rate, respects source `robots.txt`, and collects no PII.
- **Vietnamese Law**: Compliant with the Cybersecurity Law 2015, Decree 13/2023/ND-CP, and Decree 80/2023/ND-CP.
- **Trademarks**: Petrolimex, PVOil, Mipec, etc. are property of their respective owners, used solely for source attribution.
